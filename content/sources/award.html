Awards

Asian Pacific Bar Association of Silicon Valley (APBASV) Corporate Diversity Award

This is the fifth year that APBASV would like to recognize and honor the corporation with the legal department that has demonstrated a strong commitment to diversity and inclusiveness.

Applications are due by August 2, 2010. Click on this link for application and more information.

The Corporate Diversity Award will be presented at APBASV's annual scholarship and diversity awards dinner on October 15, 2010 at the Four Seasons Hotel in Palo Alto, CA.

 

Past Corporate Award Recipients

2009 Nokia
2008 Sun Microsystems, Inc.
2007 PG&E
2006 Intel Corporation

 

Asian Pacific Bar Association of Silicon Valley (APBASV) Law Firm Diversity Award

Applications are due by August 2, 2010. Click on this link for application and more information.

APBASV would like to recognize and honor the law firm that has demonstrated a strong commitment to diversity and inclusiveness in the workplace and in its outside endeavors. The Law Firm Diversity Award will be presented at APBASV's annual scholarship and diversity awards dinner on October 15, 2010 at the Four Seasons Hotel in Palo Alto, California.
