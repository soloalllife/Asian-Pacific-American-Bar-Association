The Social Committee sponsors social and networking events in order to promote both personal and professional relationships among APBASV members and other minority bar organizations. In the past, the Committee has organized student-attorney mixers, planned happy hour gatherings, and co-hosted picnics with other minority bar organizations. The Committee generally schedules events on a quarterly basis.

 

Welcome Message

Dear APBASV Members,

We would like to welcome you to the Social Committee. Joining the Committee is a great way to network with local judges and attorneys, develop leadership skills, and keep informed on the latest issues in the APA legal community. In the past year, the Committee has organized happy-hour events and bocce ball outings. This fall, the Committee is assisting with the coordination of the NAPABA Affiliates picnic. The Committee intends to diversify the events in the future, such as possibly attending a San Jose Sharks game. Come and get involved! For more information about the Social Committee, please contact Matthew Chen or Dennis Chin.

All the best,
