Comprised of past APABASV presidents and long serving Board Members the SAC focuses its efforts on advising the Board in key strategic initiatives, including the following four general areas:

1. Strategic Planning: planning for the future of APABASV and as well as key fundraising efforts for APABASV.

2. Nominations and Elections.

3. Leadership Development: SAC partners with the board and local affiliates in developing the future leaders of APABASV.

4. Outreach: serve as ambassadors for APABASV in the local communities and other minority bar associations.
