The Education Committee develops and coordinates seminars on topics of interest to the APABA membership. APABA is an approved State Bar of California provider of Minimum Continuing Legal Education.

The Education Committee holds an annual APA Judges Luncheon providing one hour MCLE for APA Judges and Attorneys. This usually occurs in the Fall (November) of the year.
