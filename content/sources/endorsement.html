Judiciary & Endorsement Committee

This committee evaluates judicial applicants and candidates of legal positions (Judges, District Attorneys, City Attorneys and Public Defenders) seeking endorsement from the Asian Pacific American Bar Association of Silicon Valley. Interested applicants and candidates should contact the current Chair, Mr. Thanh Ngo to initiate the process.
